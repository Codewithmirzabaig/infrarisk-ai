# EDA 02 – Infrastructure Risk Analysis

## Objectives
- Understand risk distribution
- Evaluate risk categories
- Identify major risk drivers

## Analysis
- Risk score distribution
- Default flag analysis
- Sovereign risk segmentation
- Construction risk analysis

## Findings
- Higher leverage increases risk score
- Construction delays strongly influence default probability
- Sovereign risk contributes materially to portfolio risk
- DSCR remains a key predictor of project performance

## Conclusion
Financial strength and construction execution are the dominant risk factors.
