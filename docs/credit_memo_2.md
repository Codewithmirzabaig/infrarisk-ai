# Credit Memo 2: Renewable Energy Infrastructure Project

## Project Overview

This credit memo evaluates a renewable energy infrastructure project using InfraRisk AI’s risk intelligence platform. The project is assessed across financial strength, macroeconomic conditions, construction performance, and sovereign risk exposure.

## Project Type

Solar Plant / Renewable Energy Infrastructure

## Key Risk Factors

- Construction delay
- Cost overrun
- Power purchase agreement risk
- Inflation risk
- Interest rate risk
- Sovereign payment risk

## Financial Metrics

| Metric | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| DSCR | Ability to cover debt service |
| LLCR | Loan-life cash flow coverage |
| PLCR | Project-life repayment capacity |
| PD | Probability of default |
| LGD | Recovery-risk severity |
| EAD | Loan exposure at default |
| EL | Expected loss |

## Model Assessment

The project is evaluated through the XGBoost credit risk engine. SHAP explainability supports transparent credit committee review by highlighting the most important drivers of project default risk.

## Forecasting

Prophet revenue forecasting estimates future project revenues and uncertainty intervals, supporting long-term infrastructure investment planning.

## Stress Testing

Monte Carlo simulation estimates downside risk under macroeconomic and operational uncertainty.

## Recommendation

Conditionally approve. The project is suitable for financing if the sponsor maintains strong contractual protection, stable off-take agreements, and sufficient reserve accounts.

## Risk Mitigation

- Secure long-term PPA
- Maintain debt service reserve account
- Monitor inflation and sovereign risk
- Use fixed-rate debt where possible
- Review project risk periodically through InfraRisk AI
