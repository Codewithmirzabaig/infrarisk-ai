# InfraRisk AI Risk Methodology

## Framework

InfraRisk AI combines:

1. Credit Risk Modeling
2. Monte Carlo Stress Testing
3. Revenue Forecasting
4. Explainable AI
5. Infrastructure Simulation

## Risk Components

### Financial Risk
- DSCR
- LLCR
- PLCR

### Construction Risk
- Delay Risk
- Cost Overrun Risk

### Sovereign Risk
- Political Stability
- Regulatory Risk

### Market Risk
- Interest Rate Risk
- Inflation Risk
- FX Risk

## Stress Testing

10,000 simulation scenarios are generated using Monte Carlo methods.

## Forecasting

Revenue forecasts generated using Prophet.

## Explainability

SHAP values identify the strongest drivers of project risk.
