# EDA 03 – Financial Metrics Assessment

## Objectives
- Review project finance metrics
- Evaluate repayment capacity
- Assess expected loss behavior

## Metrics Studied
- DSCR
- LLCR
- PLCR
- PD
- LGD
- EAD
- Expected Loss

## Findings
- Strong DSCR correlates with lower default rates
- LLCR and PLCR provide additional repayment insight
- Expected loss increases significantly under stressed scenarios

## Conclusion
Project finance ratios remain effective predictors of infrastructure credit quality.
